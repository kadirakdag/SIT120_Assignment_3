What is LUDUS?

LUDUS is a centralised gaming base, where information about the most beloved games to the unique and rare is shared. With the platform growing, LUDUS' aim is to bring all the information needed about a game to you directly.

How do I keep up with the latest updates to LUDUS?

To find updates to LUDUS, navigate to the latest games page and the community page to be notified of the newest and upcoming changes.

How do I find information about a game?

At LUDUS, navigate to the reviews page, articles page and/or latest games page to see the latest information on videogames. If a videogame is not there, don't you wory, we are likely developing a high quality database for that game right now!
